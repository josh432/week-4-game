Crystals Collector

Game Instructions: Click on the crystals and try to match the number!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four magical crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes ABOVE the random number.

The value of each magical crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.

Hint: Listen to Phil: "Don't lose my number!" Keep track of each crystal's value!
